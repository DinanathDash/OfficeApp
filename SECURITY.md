## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of OfficeApp seriously. If you have discovered a security vulnerability, we appreciate your help in disclosing it to us in a responsible manner.

1.  **Do not open an issue on GitHub.** Publicly logging a vulnerability can put all users at risk.
2.  **Email the maintainer.** Please send the details of the vulnerability to the project maintainer (dinanathdash).
3.  **Provide details.** Include as much information as possible to help us reproduce and fix the issue.

We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours and provide a timeline for a fix. We appreciate your efforts to improve the security of OfficeApp.
